Our Services

Life Insurance

You care about life’s important moments. We care about protecting them. Life Insurance can help give your family peace of mind when they need it the most. It can be used to replace your income, cover expenses, pay off debt or leave a legacy. Most importantly, it can provide security for your family's financial future.

Valuable Papers Insurance

Valuable Papers Insurance is specifically designed to protect businesses from the complex process of replacing damaged or lost valuable paper records. it compensates policyholders for the cost it would take to replace the papers or at their monetary value. Valuable papers insurance is particularly relevant for businesses that heavily rely on critical documents for their operations, such as legal firms, financial institutions, healthcare organizations, or educational institutions. It can also be valuable for individuals who possess important personal documents with high sentimental or financial value.

Business Insurance

Transforming risk into advantage with Business Insurance. No matter what industry you are in, your company faces risk every day. That's why TrustShield offers a wide range of business insurance options, designed to identify and reduce those risks. Beyond managing claim costs, we help companies prepare for risk and recover faster after an unexpected event. Managing the total cost of risk creates a competitive advantage, helping you protect and grow your business.

Auto Insurance

Discover car insurance that fits the way you want to live. We’re here to protect the car you love. And if you have a motorcycle, snowmobile, RV or ATV, we can cover that, too. We provide financial coverage for vehicle owners in the event of accidents, theft, damage, or other incidents involving their automobiles. It is designed to mitigate the financial burden that may arise from repairs, medical expenses, liability claims, or the loss of a vehicle.

Assets Insurance

Insuring your assets is a sound risk management strategy. Asset insurance passes the risk of loss or damage to property from your business to the insurance company. This represents a safety net that reduces the financial impact of loss or damage to expensive fixed and movable assets. That is why we are here to protects various types of tangible property and assets from damage, loss, or theft. It provides financial reimbursement or replacement value in the event of covered perils, helping individuals and businesses protect their valuable assets.

Money Insurance / Cash Insurance

There is considerable risk involved in handling cash and negotiable instruments, be it at the office, at the bank, at the place of disbursement or while in transit. Thus, this IGI Policy is extremely useful for those who handle and transact cash in large volumes everyday. Money insurance is essential for businesses that handle significant amounts of cash, such as retail stores, restaurants, banks, or cash-in-transit companies. It helps protect against financial losses due to theft, loss, or damage of money.
